About

My Story

Hi there, I'm Lisa, currently based in Union, Oregon. With a background in psychology and a passion for continuous learning, I've embarked on a journey in UX design with a focus on Frontend Development. My career path has been shaped by diverse experiences in customer service and accounting, where I honed my attention to detail, organizational skills, and customer empathy. In my UX design journey, I've cultivated strong researching and interviewing skills, utilizing tools like Figma, Adobe XD, and Microsoft Office to craft intuitive user experiences. I thrive on collaboration and am eager to expand my expertise by working alongside other UX designers in a dynamic environment. Beyond my professional pursuits, I find joy in exploring the outdoors year-round. Whether it's hiking and fishing in the summer or skiing in the winter, I cherish every moment spent in nature. I also have a passion for raising livestock and treasure quality time with my family. I'm excited about the opportunity to contribute my skills and enthusiasm to a team that values growth, creativity, and user-centric design.
